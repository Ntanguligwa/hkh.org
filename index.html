Hatua kwa Hatua translates to Step by step. We are a registered organization under the Non-Governmental Organizations Act, 2002 to operate in Tanzania, Eastern Africa.

Our core objective is to empower women and youth to overcome health and socioeconomic challenges through transformative programs that promote health equity, economic independence, and social empowerment.

üìåOur Call to Action:

  3. To advocate for climate change adaptation and resilience, focusing on empowering women and youth to address the impacts of climate change and build sustainable futures.
