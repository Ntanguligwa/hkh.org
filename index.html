Hatua kwa Hatua translates to Step by step. We are a Youth led registered organization under the Non-Governmental Organizations Act, 2002 to operate in Tanzania, Eastern Africa.

Our core objective is to empower women and youth to overcome health and socioeconomic challenges through transformative programs that promote health equity, economic independence, and social empowerment.

üî≠ Our Vision

To build healthier and economically empowered communities without compromising the rights of women and youth.

üåç Our Mission

To empower and inspire women and youth to become leaders of transformative changes in their communities through impactful communication strategies and social behavior change interventions that promote health equity and create opportunities for economic independence and social empowerment.

üìå Strategic Objectives

  1. To improve the health and well-being of women and youth through targeted interventions that promote access to quality healthcare, healthy lifestyles, and health education.
  2. To enhance the livelihoods of women and youth by providing opportunities for economic empowerment, skills development, and financial independence.
  3. To advocate for climate change adaptation and resilience, focusing on empowering women and youth to address the impacts of climate change and build sustainable futures.
